PIAIC

PRESIDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presidential initiative for Artificial Inteligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali and Zia Khan to help promote education,research and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like


  • Cloud Native Computing

  • Artificial Intelligence

    •

  • Blockchain

  • Internet of Things

    •

Cloud Native Computing :

The Cloud Natice Computing program prepares the students to program open source technologies.Linux,Dockers,Kubernetes,JavaScript,Node.js,React and React Native while introducing the students to development of Microsevices and software development techniques on clooud platform like AWS.

Artificial Inteligence :

The Artificial Intelligence of PIAIC takes the student through the foundation of AI technology, via teaching programing in open source languages like Python,Numpy,Pandas,Tensorflow 2.0 and flask and teaching deploying practical AI solutions and cases, and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.

Blockchain

the main emphasis of Blockchain program is to understand blockchain like Etherium and Hyperledger(fabric),and to program in languages such as Solidity,Javascript on frameworks like Node.js.The programe also introduces them to development of Distributed Apps (DApps),framework like Web3 and Truffle, and in depth understanding of Blockchain as-a-service (BaaS), how to offer and develop Intial Coin Offering(ICO) of crypto token.

Internet of Things

The Internet of things introduces the students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, Web Assembly,Hyper, Diesel,Google Assistant and Kubernetes while making them efficient in developing Micro services for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud and voice computing for device control.
